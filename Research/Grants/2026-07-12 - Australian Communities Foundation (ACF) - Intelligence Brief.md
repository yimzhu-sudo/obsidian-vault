## 📌 Notes & Caveats

- **ACF does not make grants directly** (except Impact Fund, which is invitation-only). All rounds are on behalf of fund holders/clients.
- **Fund holder anonymity:** Many choose to give anonymously. ACF facilitates — you may not know the ultimate donor.
- **Reporting:** Streamlined via Grant Partner Portal (GrantInterface) — low admin burden.
- **Future Fund option:** OZHARVEST could establish its own endowment fund via ACF for long-term sustainability (low-cost, ethical investment pool).
