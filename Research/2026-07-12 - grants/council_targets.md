# SmartyGrants Council Subdomain Target List for OZHARVEST Grant Monitoring

**Compiled:** 11 July 2026
**Validated:** 11 subdomains tested and confirmed active
**Total Targets:** 58 council subdomains across VIC, NSW, WA, SA, QLD
**Purpose:** Monthly cron crawl to surface food/waste/sustainability/community grants aligned with OZHARVEST programs (FEAST, NEST, NOURISH, food rescue, food waste diversion)

---

## Priority Tier Definitions

| Tier | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| **🥇 TIER 1 — HIGH** | Explicit food relief / food waste / organics / sustainability / community food / nutrition education grant streams. OZHARVEST has operations/partnerships in LGA. |
| **🥈 TIER 2 — MEDIUM** | Strong community grants with sustainability / environment / wellbeing / community development streams where food programs fit. Metro/regional hubs with OZHARVEST presence. |
| **🥉 TIER 3 — WATCH** | General community grants (small/quick response). Only pursue if OZHARVEST has local partner/project. Low yield but low effort to monitor. |

## 📋 Crawl Configuration for Cron Job

```yaml
# Monthly crawl config
schedule: "0 2 1 * *"  # 1st of month, 2am UTC (12pm AEST)
targets:
  tier1: 18   # Crawl every month
  tier2: 24   # Crawl every month
  tier3: 16   # Crawl quarterly (Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct)
  
crawl_pattern:
  - "/"                           # Landing page (lists current/upcoming rounds)
  - "/{grant-slug}"               # Individual grant pages (discovered from landing)
  
filters:
  include_keywords:
    - "food"
    - "waste"
    - "organics"
    - "FOGO"
    - "sustainab"
    - "circular"
    - "community food"
    - "food relief"
    - "food security"
    - "nutrition"
    - "cooking"
    - "FEAST"
    - "NEST"
    - "NOURISH"
    - "community kitchen"
    - "food hub"
    - "food rescue"
    - "capacity building"
  exclude_keywords:
    - "individual"
    - "artist"
    - "art"
    - "festival"
    - "event"
    - "travel"
    - "sport"
    - "recreation"
    - "heritage"
    - "building"
    - "capital works"
    - "asset"
    - "traffic"
    - "parking"

output_format: "telegram_briefing"
delivery: "origin"  # This chat
model: "deepseek/deepseek-v4-flash:floor"
```
